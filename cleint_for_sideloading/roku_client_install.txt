This method is for installing app files (.zip) you build or download from sources like GitHub. It requires a computer on the same Wi-Fi network as your Roku.

Step 1: Enable Developer Mode on Your Roku
Using your Roku remote, press the following buttons in sequence, one at a time:

Press Home 3 times

Press Up 2 times

Press Right 1 time

Press Left 1 time

Press Right 1 time

Press Left 1 time

Press Right 1 time

If done correctly, a "Developer Settings" screen will appear. Follow the prompts to agree to the terms and set a password. Your Roku will then restart.

Step 2: Access the Developer Dashboard on Your Computer

On your Roku, go to Settings > Network > About to find its IP address (e.g., 192.168.1.5).

On a computer connected to the same Wi-Fi network, open a web browser and type: http://[Roku-IP-Address]:8080. Replace [Roku-IP-Address] with your Roku's actual IP (e.g., http://192.168.1.5:8080).

A login prompt will appear. Enter rokudev as the username and the password you set in step 1.

Step 3: Install the App (Sideload)

In the "Development Application Installer" page, click Upload.

Select the .zip file of the app you want to install from your computer.

Click Install with zip.
Once the process is complete, the new app will launch on your Roku.
